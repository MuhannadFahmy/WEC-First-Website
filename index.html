What is WEC?

The Western Engineering Competition is the largest undergraduate engineering competition in Western Canada, with 200-300 competitors from attending the 5 day annual competition held since 1985. These competitors must first win their school’s engineering competition to qualify for WEC, and represent the best engineering students from the 10 Western Engineering Student Societies’ Team (WESST) member schools.

Our Goal

We empower students to take their skills outside the classroom and apply them to unique and memorable challenges. We dare competitors to push the bounds of what is possible by thinking outside the box.

Sponsorship
